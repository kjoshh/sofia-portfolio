Hi, I'm Sofia Cartuccia, a mixed-media artist.

Sometimes they call me Sybil.

About

My academic background is in languages and arts management.

I shoot film as a ritual act, the beauty of a sacred, repeated gesture.

My creative process is inherently intuitive and a space for constant experimentation, where every lesson is drawn from the unpredictable. I embrace imperfection as a way to honor the fragility of our internal landscapes.
